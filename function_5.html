VersaLog

All the problems that you have during the game are logged to a file along the path VRChat => Logs => VersaLog-mm-dd-yyy.txt Send it to us and we will solve your problem.

BlockPortals

Are you tired of someone spamming portals in the world and your friends are constantly getting into them? ban portals in the world and everyone will be saved.

SecureServer

Our client is tied to the server, which ensures a secure and encrypted connection.

ToggleJump

Do you dance in the VRChat? are you tired of being able to jump by accident? turn off the jump or turn it on where you can't jump.

ToggleMove

Are you still dancing and now you are randomly moving? then turn that off too!

ToggleChair

Well, almost everything is perfect for dancing, but then you accidentally sat on a chair? and the whole dance is all ruined? Then let's disable the ability to sit on chairs!
